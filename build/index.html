Nov 2013

Web Creative Lead

Empowered with setting the online design direction for the University of Melbourne, Australia’s most prestigious university, I radically modernised the University’s online user experience through the creation of web.unimelb.edu.au - a best practice living style guide and design system. Our unique approach, which combines functional yet elegant design with high quality code and a focus on meeting user needs through high quality content, has been admired by many leaders in the design and development field.

The design system has generated massive efficiencies for the University by making it simple for a dispersed group of web professionals to creating functional, accessible and highly usable websites while retaining central control of all major branding assets. The design system has saved the university millions of dollars in design and development work, implemented a consistent approach to UX across the digital presence (of over 2 millions pages) and ensured that it is seen as a benchmark for other universities to strive for in the digital space.

I was also able to use the design system to:

  • Increase sign up rate on the Open Day 2014 website by over 30%. This result was achieved by championing a major shift from a stakeholder driven approach, to an approach focusing on meeting user needs (and perhaps even delighting them a little bit).
  • Invent, prototype and pitch a new digital publishing platform for the University on which it could increase its engagement with the public. The strength of my prototype meant that we were given a significant development budget and a mandate to build a platform on behalf of and for the entire university.
